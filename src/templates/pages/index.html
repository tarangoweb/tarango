Behind every great design is a great story.

TARANGO is a creative consultancy that specializes exclusively in executive story decks.

We work closely with our clients to find the story that is not only impactful, but genuinely authentic. Our work is often described as simple, clean and beautiful, but it is always the principles of compelling and persuasive that guide us.

We are professionals in storytelling and transformation. And we hold quality design and collaboration as our key ethos.

At Tarango, we understand that business leaders have the need to communicate powerful ideas but are often without time, resources or capabilities to transform them into a visual story. We partner with you to provide the strategic framework and high-end quality design not often found in the presentation realm. The result is a powerful story deck that promotes persuasive narrative and heightened audience connection.

Building the components of a powerful presentation

01 Discover

  • Background and vision
  • Brand assessment
  • Executive and stakeholder intake
  • Question, discuss, challenge

02 Explore

  • Concept definition
  • Design exploration
  • Illustration and data visualization

03 Build

  • Production
  • Rehearse
  • Polish
  • Present

Linda is founding partner and leads all client collaborations at Tarango.

For nearly 10 years, Linda Tarango served as project manager for Steve Jobs' legendary keynote presentations that introduced iPod, iTunes, MacBook Air, iPhone, and iPad to the world. Prior to her work at Apple, Linda managed advertising projects for ABC Television, Disney, AT&T and Cisco. She works with a trusted and deeply talented group of creative partners that bring decades of experience.
