Jawbone - Later Development

An evolving brand and a new product launch with channel communication needs.

As marketing communications and brand identity evolved at Jawbone, we adapted in the presentation realm. Mini Jambox was a new product launch and the sales channel was in need of communication tools. We leveraged the new look being used in marketing and provided the sales team with high-end slide visuals that were customizable yet on-brand. The slides provided the channel with tools to speak to key product features, tech specifications, and included customizable charts.
