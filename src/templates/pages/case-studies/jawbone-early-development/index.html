Jawbone - Early Development

Strategic visualizations for high-stakes audiences.

We began work with Jawbone to improve the visual storytelling at quarterly Board of Directors meetings. We produced high volume quarterly decks which reflected contributions from the entire executive leadership team and quickly became the go-to source for strategic visualization of key concepts when talking to investors and other high-stakes audiences. The attached samples represent the early development work we did for Jawbone in the wearables category. We developed a simple yet effective visual identity that could be used consistently and effectively across presentation needs and allowed the CEO to customize and mix-and-match a growing bank of slide visuals for ongoing business communication needs. In addition to investor deck slides, we collaborated with Jawbone to produce product launch decks and templated material for the sales channel.
