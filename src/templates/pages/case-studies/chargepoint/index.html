Chargepoint

Proving once again that less is more.

ChargePoint was hosting an all-day media event to help press representatives better understand the electric vehicle charging category. We collaborated with the executive team to provide a consistent look and feel across presenter materials and worked individually with CEO, CCO, CMO and Head of Technology to address a wide range of topics including company history, vision moving forward and category misconceptions. We accomplished a new standard of presenting within the organization that proved once again, that less is more. Executive leaders could now break free of bullet points and text heavy PowerPoint slides. Our presentation design received high praise from the presenters, internal design department and event attendees.
