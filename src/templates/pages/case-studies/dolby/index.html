Dolby

A Gartner Symposium Keynote.

Dolby was a well-established client when this project came to us. We had been working for a number of years on their annual Worldwide Sales and Marketing Conference, an event that brought together hundreds of employees and included thought leadership presentations of 5-7 executive team members. We developed a trusted relationship that grew into additional opportunities throughout the organization when high-stakes presentations were needed. This deck reflects the collaboration of 3-5 working sessions with the EVP of the Dolby Voice product. He wanted to stand out from other presenters with a more story-driven presentation and wanted to lead with the Dolby brand philosophy. We leveraged a recently produced corporate anthem video and customized slides to build a deck that spoke to Dolby's history and how a relatively new product line fit into the overall brand promise.
